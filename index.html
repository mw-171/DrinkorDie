So, how does it work?

Our water alert uses a simple, user-friendly system that will guarantee even the most dehydrated person an improved hydrating schedule.
Simply attach your water bottle to the cylinder and wait for the timer to do its magic. If our specialty sensor systems do not detect movement in 1 hour, the built-in timer in the base/coaster will buzz, reminding you to drink. It will only stop buzzing when you take a drink, ensuring that you remain hydrated and healthy. The intervals between the reminders are adjustable and can be changed to fit your personal needs and schedule so you can stay hydrated all the time. The DRINK. or DIE system also includes a built-in LCD that records the last time you took a sip of water.
